About AyasofyaCharity

Inspired by the historical legacy of Hagia Sophia, AyasofyaCharity is a transparent blockchain-based charity platform powered by Pi Network. We connect donors directly with those in need through secure, verifiable transactions.

Our mission is to leverage blockchain technology to create a new standard of transparency in charitable giving, ensuring that every donation makes a measurable impact.
